Grilled Cheese Sandwich with Bacon, Tomato, and Ham

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 2 slices of ham
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 slices of bread

Instructions

1. Cook the bacon in a skillet over medium heat until crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
2. Heat the ham in the skillet for about 30 seconds per side until slightly browned.
3. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
4. Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, in a skillet over medium heat.
5. Layer a slice of cheese, ham, bacon, tomato slices, and another slice of cheese on top.
6. Top with the second slice of bread, buttered side up.
7. Cook until the bottom slice is golden brown, then flip and cook the other side until golden and cheese is melted.
8. Remove from heat, slice in half, and serve warm.
